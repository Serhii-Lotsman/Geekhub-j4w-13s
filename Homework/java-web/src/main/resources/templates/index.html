How to encrypt a message?

Here is a regular message encryptor, but with an interesting additional tool.
You can not only encrypt your message, but also decrypt it. Brilliant, I know.
Briefly about the functionality:
  • On the encrypt/decrypt tab, you can encrypt or decrypt messages with several (Caesar and Vigenere) ciphers.
  • You can toggle between encrypt/decrypt and switch between encryptors for now.
  • On the history tab, you can view the message history
  • You can also access the history of all messages that have been used.
  • View the entire history of messages
  • View the number of messages encrypted/decrypted by this or that cipher
  • by date, by cipher and much more
So, encrypt as much as you want!
